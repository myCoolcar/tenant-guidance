What's gone wrong?

🔧
Repairs are not being fixed →

Your landlord is legally required to fix heating, hot water, plumbing, and structural issues. Find out what they must do.

 💷
My deposit has not been returned →

Deposits must be protected in a government scheme. Learn how to challenge unfair deductions and claim compensation.

 🏠
I am being asked to leave →

A landlord cannot evict you without following the correct legal process. Understand your rights and what to do next.

 🚪
My landlord entered without notice →

Landlords must give at least 24 hours' written notice before entering. Repeated entry without notice may be illegal.

 🧾
I have been charged an unfair fee →

Many letting fees are banned under the Tenant Fees Act 2019. Find out if what you were charged is legal.

 🤔
I'm not sure what my problem is →

Start with our overview of tenant rights. Or use the assistant below to describe your situation in your own words.
